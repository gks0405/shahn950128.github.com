Identity, dynamic labels, and iconography for Blue Terminal energy information network.

Blue Terminal is an ongoing project building an energy information network. They aggregate a feed of energy news, and visualize complex research data.

We designed an identity that reflects the data-visualization nature of Blue Terminal and their focus on energy research. Created visual language works flexibly in print and digital applications, and encompasses a dynamic labeling system.
