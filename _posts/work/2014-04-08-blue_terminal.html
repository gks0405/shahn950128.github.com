Product design, UI/UX and art-direction for Blue Terminal, an energy information network.

Blue Terminal is an ongoing project building an energy information network. They aggregate a feed of energy news and visualize complex research data.

This part of my work with Blue Terminal covered product design and development of the user interface. Design of the website evolved through focused stages: fundraising, user sign-up, functionality testing and public launch.

The other major part of the project included a dynamic identity and an iconographic system.
