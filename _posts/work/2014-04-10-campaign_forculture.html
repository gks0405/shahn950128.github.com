Identity, illustration and project art-direction for Campaign for Culture in British Columbia.

Campaign for Culture is a grassroots organization that changed liquor laws in British Columbia.

Project design needed to reflect the professional nature of the campaign, yet remain friendly and accessible to people not aware of the issues. This resulted in a clean typographic approach combined with a series of simple, but colorful illustrations.

Custom illustrations were created for each campaign stage and specific sections of the website.
