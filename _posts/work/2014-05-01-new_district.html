Identity, art-direction, and product design for New District, a marketplace for BC Wineries.

New District is an online marketplace and lifestyle publication focused on British Columbia’s diverse wine industry.

Our goal was to build a place where people could come to learn about the industry and buy great wine. This led to a clean design with spacious, comfortable layouts, magazine-style typography, art-directed articles, and custom, on-location photography.

New District’s wine selection is instantly accessible through live search. Project interface flexibly adjusts to varied devices without compromising design or usability.
