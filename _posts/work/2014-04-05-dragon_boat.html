A series of posters for the annual Vancouver Dragon Boat Festival.

Dragon Boat Festival is an annual event in Vancouver, BC. This festival spans a broad range of events, for which the organizers commissioned a series of posters.

Poster design was based on the vibrant styles of traditional dragonboats. Outside of typography all illustrations are hand drawn, with color added digitally.
